Project # and Type PROJECT #2: Website to help others Specific Project Title “Website for Living Guide in Kyoto for International Students”

Reason(s) for the project • Most of us have faced difficulties in the initial days related to how to travel from one place to another place using a train. • Most of us have struggled how to find destination places in Google Maps and related to its other information like time and cost. • In the initial days, we did not know which train is right to get to our correct destination. So, sometimes we went to another place and got lost there. • We have faced many problems taking tickets every day to go to a part-time job and also we did not have any idea about the ICOCA card. • We did not know how to manage household garbage and the right days and place to throw it. • Also, we did not have the basic knowledge to stay here in Kyoto.

Desired project scope and outcome(s) Mainly, this project (website) will help newcomers students in Kyoto in the following areas: • Video and descriptive information on: - How to search for a destination place on Google Maps and its cost and time. - How to use Bus, Train to travel from one place to another? - How to use an ICOCA card.

• Descriptive information on: - How to manage garbage. - How to buy daily needed foods. - Information for most visiting places in Kyoto and a travel guide.

• Embedded YouTube videos for more information from other sources. • Useful links.

Associated Sustainable Development Goal(s) if any Goal 9: Industry, Innovation and Infrastructure In Japan, It is very difficult to get information if we don’t know about the Japanese language. All information like hoarding boards, Posters, and Map Guides are in the Japanese language. So, by doing this small project it will help international students to get the correct information. Thus, our project will help to reduce the inequality of access to information and facilitates access to information about living guide.
