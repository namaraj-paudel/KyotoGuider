HOW TO BUY DAILY NEEDED FOODS

1. Visit a Supermarket:

  Supermarkets are the most popular places in Japan to buy daily food items. Many supermarkets offer a wide selection of fresh produce, meats, dairy products, fish, and other food items.


2. Visit a Convenience Store:

  Convenience stores (or “conbini”) are a great place to buy food for daily meals. Most convenience stores offer a variety of ready-made meals, snacks, and beverages.


3. Visit a Local Grocery Store:

  Local grocery stores are a great place to find fresh and affordable produce, meats, and other food items. Many local stores offer a wide range of local and imported items.


4. Visit a Farmers Market:

  Farmers markets are a great place to find fresh and locally produced food items. Many farmers markets offer a variety of seasonal produce, meats, and other food items.


5. Visit a Department Store:

  Department stores are a great place to find imported food items. Many department stores have a food court with a variety of food stalls and restaurants.


6. Look for stores that offer discounts and promotions:

  Many stores in Kyoto offer discounts and promotions for international students. Ask around for stores that offer discounts for international students or search online for stores that offer special discounts.


7. Join a student discount program:

  Many stores in Kyoto offer student discount programs that offer special discounts on food and other items. Ask around or search online for stores with student discounts.


8. Shop at local markets:

  Local markets are usually much cheaper than supermarkets and usually carry fresh, local produce. Shopping at local markets can help you save money while still getting the food you need.


9. Buy in bulk:

  Buying food in bulk is usually much cheaper than buying smaller amounts. Look for stores that offer bulk discounts or search online for stores that offer special discounts on bulk purchases.


10. Shop at discount stores:

  Discount stores are usually much cheaper than regular supermarkets and offer a wide range of food items at discounted prices. Search online for discount stores in Kyoto and plan your shopping trips accordingly.
